We are a development oriented technology organization with a vision to provide affordable, integrated and smart ICT tools for organisations working at BoP levels.

Our Solutions




Non-profits
Still juggling with spreadsheets to collate data for different reports? Get in touch for a real time monitoring system to generate reports and share them with your donors.

CSR led initiatives
Coordinating with different agencies to get reports in time is a BIG struggle! Explore our impact assessment platform to measure progress of all your projects with stunning dashboards.


Microfinance Institutions
Recording transactions and activities on paper is past! Reach us for our mobile based finance inclusion platform which helps you go digital and also integrates recent IOT developments - e-KYC, Biometric etc.

Other Social Enterprises
Wondering what else we can offer? Give us a shout and we will be glad to help you evaluate applications to enhance your processes. Eg: Community outreach tools, SHG accounting systems etc.
